Shipping & documentation guidelines

Required email details (in the body, explicitly): Brand · FBA ID · Pickup Date · No. of Pallets / No. of Boxes · Carrier.

Mandatory attachments: Bill of Lading (BOL); pallet labels — 4 copies of each individual pallet label; box labels — organized and attached in ascending order.

Box list format: list each box individually, in ascending order, one per row. Never group multiple box numbers into a single row.

Highlights: boxes needing relabeling must be highlighted in the email body (even if updated labels were sent separately). Cross-brand boxes mixed into the shipment must also be highlighted.

UPS shipments: send UPS labels as one label (do not break into FBA + UPS); share the total weight (required to schedule pickup); coordinate across brands so all UPS boxes pick up on one day.

Single orders from a Jumbo box: mention Brand, Box #, Item, FNSKU, and Carrier (UPS/FedEx/USPS). Use the "Single order" note field in the brand block.

Tracking: update every shipment on the shared Excel file (Pallet/Boxes – CBM vs Cost), even one Jumbo box.

Timing: email Amazon shipment pickups at least 3 days before the pickup date. The app warns you if the pickup date is too close.
